Spaghetti Carbonara

  • spaghetti
  • bacon
  • eggs
  • cheese
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. heat water
  2. while waiting the water, cook the bacon with oil
  3. mix eggs and cheese
  4. cook spaghetti in the boiling water
  5. toss spaghetti with bacon
  6. add the egg mixture
  7. add salt and pepper to your spaghetti
