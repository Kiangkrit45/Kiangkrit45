Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is an Italian main course consisting of Spaghetti with bacon and a creamy sauce made from eggs, cheese and pepper.

I like how normal and comforting the dish is, yet it tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • bacon
  • eggs
  • cheese
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Methods

  1. 🔥 heat water 💧
  2. while waiting the water, cook the bacon 🥓 with oil
  3. mix eggs 🥚 and cheese 🧀
  4. cook spaghetti 🍝 in the boiling water
  5. toss spaghetti 🍝 with bacon 🥓
  6. add the egg 🥣 mixture
  7. add salt 🧂 and pepper to your spaghetti 🍝
